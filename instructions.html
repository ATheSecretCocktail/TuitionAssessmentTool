The Secret Cocktail® Tuition Assessment Tool Instructions

Description

This tool is designed to help you explore your tuition options.

All mandatory costs must have non-negative number values.

Don’t forget your report! Make your selections and replace the sample values with your own. Once you are done you can download a report for your records from the Report tab.

Navigational Buttons

When you are creating a course and filling in inputs, the buttons on the bottom labeled "Next" and "Back" in blue will allow you to navigate through the process as intended.

Shortcut Buttons

When you are inputting values on the forms, there will be buttons that will help you fill out empty fields quickly with placeholders, select/de-select all checkboxes, and even empty all the fields on the page so you can restart inputting the values for the page with just a click of a button (Remember: "Empty Fields" only resets the fields on the page!). These buttons will be colored red and be on the right side of the form pages.

Checkboxes

Use the checkboxes to select which supplies you intend to include in your Optional Costs. Only checked items will be included in calculations.
